About Us

At NDataViso, we understand that in today's data-driven world, managing and harnessing the power of information is critical for success. As a leading provider in web and database management solutions, we specialize in helping businesses unlock the full potential of their data assets.

We concentrate in providing software development, implementation and consulting services in Microsoft with superior focus on .Net, database and BI Reporting. Our skills develop to a number of applications, databases and BI Reporting including SQL Server, SSIS, SSRS, Microsoft Power BI, SSAS, Data warehouse (DW) design/development, MS Excel/VBA/Access and Tableau reporting. Also, Microsoft AZURE cloud administration and maintenance, Migration from on-premises to Azure, optimization of resources and cost reduction.
NDataViso wants to support its customers in these developments, with knowledge, expertise and the right people in the right place at the right time!

Our Team Values

At NDataViso, our team is a dynamic combination of implementation specialists, support experts as well as integration and development professionals. Also, we endeavor to empower businesses, guaranteeing their development and success through our cooperative approach and advanced solutions. Our team has an experience of 15+ years implementing software solutions.
